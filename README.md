## 🌠 About the Background Image
The beautiful background featured in this project's styling is a real photo of 
the [Galaxy Cluster Abell 370](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Abell_370).

* The cluster contains several hundred galaxies tied together by the mutual pull of gravity.
* It acts as a huge lens in space that magnifies and stretches images of background galaxies like
a funhouse mirror.
* The brightest and largest galaxies are the yellow-white, massive, elliptical galaxies containing
many hundreds of billions of stars each.
* Spiral galaxies have younger populations of stars and are bluish.
* Mysterious-looking arcs of blue light are distorted images of remoted galaxies behind the cluster.
* Photo Credit: [NASA](https://science.nasa.gov/asset/hubble/galaxy-cluster-abell-370-2/), 
[ESA](https://esahubble.org/images/heic1711a/), and J. Lotz 
and the HFF Team ([STScI](https://archive.stsci.edu/prepds/frontier/)).
